Shlurdle!

N words

This is a variant of Wordle: guess the secret word.

It plays by Absurdle rules: it's always the unluckiest word consistent with previous clues.

It only remembers the last guesses, so that's how much consistency you get.

Words have no rpeatd letrs.

This is not a bitwise challenge game: its state is 21*N bits. I hope you had fun anyway.
